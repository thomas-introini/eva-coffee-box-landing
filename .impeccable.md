## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are home coffee enthusiasts in Italy who brew with moka, espresso, and filter methods and are arriving from marketing/tracking campaigns.
Their context is a focused purchase moment on a dedicated landing page, often on mobile, where they need to pick two grind options and complete checkout quickly.
The core job to be done is a fast, confident purchase with low friction and clear guidance.

### Brand Personality
Brand personality: artisanal, honest, modern.
Voice and tone should feel warm, clear, and trustworthy, highlighting craft quality and freshness without aggressive sales pressure.
The emotional goal is trust plus warmth throughout selection and checkout.

### Aesthetic Direction
Visual direction is clean organic editorial in light mode only.
Use warm coffee tones (earthy browns, creams, terracotta-style accents) with restrained contrast and refined typography.
Interface should feel premium but approachable: generous spacing, calm hierarchy, and concise copy.

### Design Principles
1. Remove friction at every step: keep the grind-selection-to-checkout flow obvious, short, and error-resistant.
2. Build confidence through clarity: make pricing, shipping, and selection state immediately understandable.
3. Express craft with restraint: use warm, organic visual cues without clutter or visual noise.
4. Keep accessibility first by default: target WCAG 2.2 AA, strong focus states, keyboard support, and color-safe communication.
5. Respect user comfort: support reduced motion and avoid non-essential animation that competes with conversion.
